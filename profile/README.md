## Fixes for Missed Market Notices

Notifications not arriving: check system permissions, battery restrictions, and app notification settings for the stock notification app. Alert too frequent: revise stock market alerts, reduce duplicate stock price alerts, or group symbols into cleaner stock watchlist alerts. Wrong target level: edit the stock price alarm and confirm the ticker, currency, and trigger direction. Delayed updates: switch networks, reopen Stock Alarm, and confirm real time stock alerts are enabled.
